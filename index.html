Synopsis

Curtis is a waveset-segment-glisson granulator. Unlike normal granulators, the plugin segments the incoming audio signal along the zero-crosses and does not apply any windowing functions that may destruct the characteristics of the original sound. The segmented snippets of audio signal are cached and played back with specified number of time and order from the different stereo positions with glissandi. This process creates a particle-like sound effects with radical transformation of the original sound by keeping the sonority of it.
